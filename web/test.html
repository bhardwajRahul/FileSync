FileSync Diagnostics

If a transfer isn't working, open this page in the affected browser and share the diagnostics with the FileSync maintainer.

You are testing FileSync from localhost. The STUN/TURN diagnostics below are not representative of what your end users will see. Some browsers (notably Firefox) deliberately restrict ICE candidate gathering for loopback addresses as a privacy measure — this is intended browser behavior, not a FileSync bug, and does not affect real deployments.

To verify the network configuration your users will actually experience, access this page at the same address they'll use to reach FileSync — your LAN IP (e.g. http://192.168.x.x/test.html) or your domain. The signaling-layer probe below is the authoritative test for FileSync's server-side functionality and works from any URL.
